FAQ
Common questions.
Does it work with my broker?
NoVo uses Tradier for live options execution and Alpaca for paper trading. Both are required. Tradier is the live brokerage — trades route through their official API. If you already have a Tradier account, you're most of the way there.
Do I need to watch the screen all day?
No. NoVo runs as three persistent Windows services that start automatically with your PC and run in the background through market hours. You can monitor everything — positions, P&L, signal state, circuit breaker status — from your phone via the mobile dashboard. The system handles entries, exits, and position management on its own.
Can it lose money?
Yes — and you should know that before buying. NoVo is an execution tool, not a guarantee. It takes trades based on configurable rules and a multi-layer signal score, and like any trading system it will have losing trades and losing days. You configure the daily max loss limit, position sizing, and stop levels. Start in paper mode, understand how the system behaves in real market conditions, and only run real capital you're prepared to risk.
What happens if my PC restarts during market hours?
All three NoVo services are registered via NSSM and configured to restart automatically. If the machine reboots, services come back up on their own within seconds. Any open position at the time of restart is picked up from your broker account on reconnect.
Is algorithmic trading via API legal?
Yes. NoVo connects to your own Tradier brokerage account through their official retail API — the same API Tradier explicitly provides for automated trading. You're placing trades in your own account. Algorithmic trading via broker APIs is fully legal for retail accounts in the US.
What does the AI actually do?
The AI (Google Gemini or Anthropic Claude — your choice, configured in your .env) acts as a visual chart reader. Every 3 minutes, NoVo generates a live SPY chart and sends it to your AI provider for a directional read. That read becomes one input into the entry signal — it won't take a long trade if the AI reads the structure as bearish. The AI doesn't make trading decisions; the algorithm does. The AI is one signal layer among several.
Can I adjust the risk settings?
Yes — everything is adjustable from the dashboard without touching code. Daily max loss, profit goal, position size, Apex Score entry threshold, TP1/TP2 levels, trailing stop behavior — all configurable live. Changes take effect on the next scan cycle. No restart required.
How does setup work?
After purchase, your zip file and license key arrive by email. Unzip to C:\Trading Algo\NoVo v.fast, rename .env.template to .env, paste your license key and API credentials, then right-click setup.ps1 and run as Administrator. The script installs Python dependencies, Redis, NSSM, registers all three services, and starts the system. The full walkthrough is in the included PDF — most people are running in under 20 minutes.
