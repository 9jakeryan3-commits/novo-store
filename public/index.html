How It Works
Execution without interference.

NoVo removes the failure modes that erode trader performance — hesitation, revenge trades, emotional exits — and replaces them with a consistent, rules-based execution framework powered by four AI layers running simultaneously.

01  ·  SIGNAL

Apex Score — Four Conviction Tiers

Three signal components — tape imbalance, RVOL, and gamma proximity — combine into a real-time Apex Score (0–100) every 5 seconds. Four conviction tiers — Confluence, Mid Override, High Conviction, Nuclear — each carry a progressively larger position size multiplier.

A dual-layer tape filter blocks spoofed moves before they reach the score. All three signals must align: score above tier threshold, AI visual bias in the correct direction, and sustained directional pressure. In FOMO or CHOP regimes, lower-conviction tiers are automatically suppressed. No partial alignment fires a trade.

02  ·  EXECUTE

MAB Router — Learns Every Fill

A Multi-Armed Bandit algorithm continuously tests three order strategies — Midpoint Passive, Chase & Replace, Aggressive Sweep — and exploits whichever produces the best fills in current conditions.

Position sizing is conviction-weighted: your Apex tier determines contract count, so Nuclear setups automatically size larger than Confluence entries from the same allocation budget. At Nuclear conviction or extreme tape velocity, passive fills are banned — the move can't be missed for the sake of price. The system gets better fills the longer it runs.

03  ·  MANAGE

9-Tier Exit Hierarchy

Exits execute in strict priority order across nine tiers — from manual overrides and EOD protocols down through hard stops, trailing exits, and profit-taking targets. Higher-priority exits always win. The system never hesitates between competing exit conditions.

TP1 sells half the position and promotes the remainder to a runner — toggle it off to skip the partial and hold the full position into TP2. TP2 closes the runner at your second target — toggle it off to let the runner trail indefinitely in trending markets. Two independent trailing stops protect open profit — a tighter runner trail for extended moves and a standard trail for normal exits — both with adjustable width and activation thresholds. Nearly every threshold is configurable live from the dashboard without a restart. Math dictates exits — not emotion.

CORE PRINCIPLE

Two Exits Are Hardcoded — By Design

The Breakeven Saver and the Structural Reversal are the only exits that cannot be adjusted from the dashboard. Both exist to enforce the same principle: spend as little time in the market as possible. When the tape rejects a peak move, you exit. When the AI reads the chart against your open position across multiple confirmed reads, you exit. These rules cannot be turned off because the only moment a trader wants to disable them is when they're in a losing position and hoping it turns around. That hope is exactly the behavior systematic execution was built to replace. Everything else is configurable. These two are not.

Real-Time  ·  Vision Sensor

The AI reads live charts and gates every single entry.

Every 3 minutes, NoVo generates a live SPY chart and sends it to your AI provider for a structural read. That directional bias is a hard gate on every entry. No bullish read, no long. No bearish read, no short.

On high-volatility tape events, it accelerates to 1-minute reads. A special Opening Bell Primer fires before the market opens to frame the session bias. Bias decays automatically — if the chart hasn't been read recently, entries are gated until a fresh read arrives.

Weekly  ·  Mastery Loop

NoVo rewrites its own trading laws every week from real outcomes.

Every Friday, NoVo compiles the full week of trade data and sends it to the AI for deep analysis. The AI identifies what worked, what failed, and why — then synthesizes those lessons into permanent trading laws that govern all future behavior.

This isn't backtesting on historical data. The system is evolving from its own live market experience, session after session. It gets sharper every week with zero input from you.

Every 5 Minutes  ·  Intuition Engine

NoVo knows what retail traders are doing right now — and trades against their mistakes.

Every 5 minutes, a dedicated AI layer synthesizes the current retail psychology state: FOMO, CAPITULATION, MOMENTUM_BUILDING, CHOP, or NEUTRAL.

When retail is chasing (FOMO) or panic-selling (CAPITULATION), that state becomes a signal input. In CHOP conditions, confluence entries are suppressed to avoid noise. NoVo trades with awareness of crowd psychology.

Continuous  ·  Systemic Resonance

FAQ
Common questions.
Does it work with my broker?
Tradier is always required — it powers the live WebSocket tick stream that feeds all signal calculations (tape imbalance, RVOL, GEX). This runs regardless of how you're trading. A minimum $5 deposit activates the stream.

For trade execution you choose your broker:

Alpaca — paper or live: Alpaca has two sets of API keys — paper keys and live keys. Paste your paper keys in Settings for simulated trading, or swap in your live keys to trade real money. That's the only change needed to switch between the two.

Tradier — live only: Tradier executes real orders regardless of any setting. Never select Tradier while in paper mode — it will trade real money.

Most users start on Alpaca paper, learn the system, then swap to Alpaca live or Tradier when ready.
Do I need to watch the screen all day?
No. NoVo runs as three persistent Windows services that start automatically with your PC and run through market hours in the background. You can monitor everything — Apex Score, live position, P&L, regime state, circuit breaker status — from your phone via the mobile dashboard. The system handles entries, exits, and position management without you watching.
What does the AI actually do?
The AI is not optional — NoVo doesn't trade without it. Four AI layers run simultaneously:

Vision Sensor: Reads a live SPY chart every 3 minutes. That read gates every entry — no valid AI read, no trade.

Intuition Engine: Every 5 minutes, detects the current retail psychology state (FOMO, CAPITULATION, MOMENTUM_BUILDING, CHOP, NEUTRAL) and adjusts entry aggressiveness accordingly.

Systemic Resonance: Continuously classifies the macro regime (RISK-ON, RISK-OFF, CHOP, MIXED) using VIX, HYG credit stress, DXY, and the Whale Sensor (institutional sweep detection).

Mastery Loop: Every Friday, reviews the full week of trade outcomes and rewrites permanent trading laws that govern future behavior. The system evolves every week.

What happens if my PC restarts during market hours?
All three NoVo services are registered via NSSM and configured to restart automatically on reboot. If the machine goes down, services come back up within seconds. Position state is persisted through the system — any open position at restart is picked up from your broker account on reconnect. No manual intervention needed.
Is algorithmic trading via API legal?
Yes. NoVo connects to your own Tradier brokerage account through their official retail API — the same API Tradier explicitly provides for automated trading. You're placing trades in your own account. Algorithmic trading via broker APIs is fully legal for retail accounts in the US.
What is the Apex Score and how does entry sizing work?
The Apex Score is a 0–100 real-time measure of setup quality, updated every 5 seconds. It synthesizes three signal components — tape imbalance, RVOL, and gamma proximity — into a single conviction number. Four tiers determine both whether an entry fires and how large the position is:

Confluence: Moderate conviction — smallest size
Mid Override: Solid setup — standard size
High Conviction: Strong alignment — elevated size
Nuclear: Maximum conviction or extreme tape event — largest size

The threshold for each tier is configurable from the dashboard.
Can I adjust the risk settings?
Yes — nearly everything is adjustable from the dashboard Risk & Allocation Matrix without touching code. Daily max loss, profit goal, position size, Apex Score entry thresholds, TP1/TP2 levels, trailing stop behavior, hard stop %, strike OTM offset — all configurable live. Changes take effect on the next scan cycle. No restart required.

Two exits are intentionally not adjustable: the Breakeven Saver and the Structural Reversal. These are hardcoded by design to enforce NoVo's core principle — minimize time in the market. When a trade peaks and rejects, you exit. When the AI reads the chart against your position after multiple confirmed reads, you exit. They cannot be disabled because they protect against the single most costly discretionary behavior: holding a losing position and waiting for a recovery that may not come. Everything else is yours to configure.
