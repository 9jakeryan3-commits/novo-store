What you see

The dealer's map — decoded

Six institutional reads on SPY, QQQ & SPX, computed off live options positioning and updated through the session. Every term is explained in plain English — you don't need a desk background to use it.

🧲

Net GEX · the regime

Positive = dealers dampen moves (grind / mean-revert). Negative = they amplify (moves extend). Know which market you're in before you enter.

🔀

Zero-Gamma flip

The price where dealer hedging flips from dampening to amplifying. Cross it and the whole character of the tape changes.

🧱

Call & Put walls

The biggest gamma strikes — the pins price gets drawn to and the levels it reacts at. Resistance above, support below.

📐

Expected move

The ±1σ range the options market is pricing for the day and the week — your realistic playing field, VIX/VXN-anchored.

⚖️

Put/Call skew

Are traders paying up for downside protection or chasing upside? The market's fear-vs-greed tell, in vol points.

📊

ATM IV & IV rank

How expensive options are right now versus their own recent range — so you know when premium is cheap or rich.

No. NoVo Analyst is market analysis and education — a structural read of conditions and probabilities. It never tells you to “buy calls here.” It shows you what the market is doing and what tends to follow; you make the calls.

The live SPY/QQQ/SPX dealer map — net GEX, the gamma flip, dealer walls, expected move, skew and IV rank — in an installable members dashboard; a written desk-note read emailed each session (the Open, the Close, and a Sunday Week Ahead); real-time ‘The Line’ level-break playbooks in the app and Discord; plus the private Analyst channels in the NoVo Discord.
How often will I hear from you?
A morning Open and an end-of-day Close each trading session, a Week Ahead on Sundays, and real-time ‘The Line’ alerts — the moment SPY breaks or reclaims a major level, with a hold-vs-break playbook and the confirmation to watch. Only when a level actually gives way, capped to one per level a day. Actionable, not a firehose.
Do I need a brokerage account or any setup?
No. NoVo Analyst is pure read — nothing to install, no broker keys to connect. You just open the dashboard and read. (Connecting your own broker so NoVo can execute is NoVo Trader, the separate automation tier.)
How is this different from NoVo Trader?
Analyst gives you the read; Trader trades it for you. Analyst ($69/mo) is the live dealer map + the written read. NoVo Trader is the full autonomous system — the same structural read, live and executing in your own broker account within the rules and risk you set. Non-custodial; it never touches your money.

NoVo's live engine reads SPY/QQQ/SPX tape, dealer positioning, and market structure through the entire session. The reads you get are that engine's view — the same structural work that drives the Trader execution tier — translated into plain language. Public options data; analysis, not signals.
