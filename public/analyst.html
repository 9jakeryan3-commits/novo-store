FAQ
Common questions.
Is this trade signals?
No. NoVo Analyst is market analysis and education — a structural read of conditions and probabilities. It never tells you to “buy calls here.” It shows you what the market is doing and what tends to follow; you make the calls.
How often will I hear from you?
A morning Open and an end-of-day Close each trading session, a Week Ahead on Sundays, and real-time ‘The Line’ alerts — the moment SPY breaks or reclaims a major level (prior-day high/low, opening range, VWAP, gamma flip), with a hold-vs-break playbook and the confirmation to watch — plus the rarer gamma-regime shift. Only when a level actually gives way, capped to one per level a day. Actionable email, not a firehose.
Do I need a brokerage account or any setup?
No. NoVo Analyst is pure email — nothing to install, no broker keys to connect. You just read. (Connecting your own broker so NoVo can execute is NoVo Trader, the separate live-execution tier.)
