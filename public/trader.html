How It Works
Your rules. Its execution.

NoVo takes the failure modes that erode trader performance — hesitation, revenge trades, emotional exits — off your plate, replacing them with a consistent, rules-based execution framework that scores every setup, sizes every entry, and manages every exit without deviation.

YOUR RULES

You set the boundaries. NoVo trades inside them.

Your main input is DTE Target — how many days to expiration NoVo trades, which sets the whole risk profile. At 0–1 DTE: maximum decay and the tightest risk, everything force-flattened before the close — nothing held overnight. At 2–5 DTE: more premium cushion and room for a trade to breathe, in exchange for overnight exposure. The finer risk controls ship pre-tuned and stay adjustable any time. You draw the lines; NoVo scores, sizes, and exits within them.

01  ·  SIGNAL

Analyst Score — grades every setup

Multiple proprietary signal components combine into a real-time Analyst Score (0–100), updated on every scan cycle. It grades the quality of each setup — and your conviction-sizing bands map that grade to position size, larger on the strongest setups from the same allocation budget.

A dual-layer tape filter blocks spoofed moves before a setup can fire. A trade only triggers on a real structure setup — a level reaction, a swept level, or a confirmed continuation once price breaks clear of the levels — with the tape confirmed. No partial alignment fires a trade, and in low-quality windows (the lunch lull, the late-session pin) the lowest-conviction setups are automatically suppressed.

02  ·  EXECUTE

Adaptive Execution Router — Learns Every Fill

A multi-armed-bandit algorithm continuously tests multiple order-execution tactics — spanning passive, adaptive, and aggressive approaches — and exploits whichever produces the best fills in current conditions.

Position sizing is conviction-weighted: your Analyst Score band sets contract count, so the strongest setups automatically size larger from the same allocation budget. At peak conviction or extreme tape velocity, passive fills are banned — the move can't be missed for the sake of price. It gets better fills the longer it runs.

03  ·  MANAGE

Multi-Tier Exit Hierarchy

Exits execute in strict priority order across multiple tiers — from manual overrides and EOD protocols down through hard stops, trailing exits, and profit-taking targets. Higher-priority exits always win. The system never hesitates between competing exit conditions.

TP1 sells half the position at your first target and promotes the remainder to a runner. Toggle it off to skip the partial and hold the full position into TP2. TP2 closes the runner — or toggle it off to let the runner trail indefinitely in trending markets.

Two independent trailing stops protect open profit: a tighter runner trail for extended moves and a standard trail for normal exits, both with adjustable width and activation thresholds. Nearly every threshold is configurable live from the dashboard without a restart. Math dictates exits — not emotion.

04  ·  YOUR CALL

Buy Calls. Buy Puts. Your read, NoVo's hands.

Two buttons on the dashboard. You spot a setup NoVo hasn't taken — hit Buy Calls or Buy Puts and it takes the trade for you: same strike selection, same conviction sizing, same broker-side stop, same multi-tier exit ladder. Not a bolted-on "manual mode" running its own code — it's NoVo's own execution engine, pointed where you tell it.

Leave it running and you're both on the tape — whoever sees it first takes it. Or hit Stop: NoVo keeps reading the market and keeps naming the setups it would have taken, and only trades when you say so. Your call, your finger, its execution.

The guardrails don't move either way. It still won't take a contract it can't fill cleanly, and never more than one position at a time.

What is the Analyst Score and how does entry sizing work?
The Analyst Score is a 0–100 real-time grade of each setup's quality, updated continuously from multiple proprietary signal components. It doesn't decide whether a trade fires — your setups (structure + tape) do — it grades the entry so your conviction-sizing bands allocate size. You set three bands: the middle band is your full allocation, sized up when the grade is high and down when it's low. Every band is configurable from the dashboard.

Nothing is hidden or hardcoded. Your stops and targets run every trade — NoVo never overrides them with a discretionary exit. The hard stop you set is the hard stop NoVo respects.

How It Works

From checkout to a live dashboard — most people are running in minutes, paper trading immediately with zero real money at risk.

You only bring your Tradier + Alpaca keys — AI is built in, so there's nothing else to configure.

1

Subscribe

Secure checkout via Stripe — monthly or yearly. Instant access, cancel any time.

2

Create your account

Head to app.novo-aitrading.app and sign up with the email you subscribed with.

3

Connect your broker keys

Paste your Tradier + Alpaca keys. They're validated against the brokers. AI is pre-configured, so there's nothing else to enter.

4

Your dashboard spins up automatically

Your own private NoVo launches automatically — no setup, no babysitting. It's ready in moments.

5

Open your dashboard

Open it from the portal in any browser, or add it to your phone's home screen. NoVo starts in paper mode — simulated trades, no real money — so there's nothing to be careful about yet. Flip to live when you're ready.

Step-by-step help lives in your portal, and support is always an email away at support@novo-aitrading.app.
